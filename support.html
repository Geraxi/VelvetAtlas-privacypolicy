Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a booking?

Browse available vehicles, yachts, villas, or jets in the app. Select your dates, choose any add-ons (like chauffeur service), and complete payment. You'll receive instant confirmation via email.

How do I cancel or modify my booking?

Go to "My Bookings" in the app, select your reservation, and choose "Cancel" or "Modify." Cancellation policies vary by partner and are shown before you book.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and debit cards. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

What if I have an issue during my rental?

Contact our 24/7 concierge service directly through the app, or email support@velvetatlas.com with your booking reference.

How does delivery work?

For supercars and some other vehicles, we offer delivery to your location. Select your delivery address during booking, and our partner will confirm the delivery time.

Is insurance included?

Insurance options are available during booking. Our rental partners offer basic and premium coverage plans. Full details are shown before you complete your reservation.

What are the driver requirements?

Requirements vary by vehicle and partner, but generally you must be 25+ years old with a valid driver's license. Exotic vehicles may require additional experience verification.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Or email us at privacy@velvetatlas.com to request account deletion.

Do you have a refund policy?

Refund policies vary by partner and are clearly stated before booking. Typically, cancellations made 48+ hours in advance receive full refunds.

Can I book for someone else?

Yes, during checkout you can specify a different driver/guest name. The person using the service must meet all requirements (age, license, etc.).

Technical Support

The app won't load or is crashing

Try these steps:

  • Force close and restart the app
  • Check for app updates in the App Store
  • Restart your device
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection

If issues persist, email us at support@velvetatlas.com with your device model and iOS version.

I can't log in to my account

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen to reset your password. If you still can't access your account, contact support.

Payment failed or was declined

Check that your card details are correct and that your bank hasn't blocked the transaction. If the issue continues, try a different payment method or contact your bank.
